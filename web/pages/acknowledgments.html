Beginning in late 2020 Health in Harmony contributed financially to development of the bbbrDB instance, and to the generalized form presented here as KlinikDB (in preparation for possible deployment in Madagascar).

In its initial stages, Dr. Kinari Webb gave major input on table elements and form design, and over the years many ASRI and BBBR doctors, nurses, pharmacists, data entry staff, and conservation staff have helped with ideas and feedback.
